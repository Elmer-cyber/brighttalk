Plan Overview

The Custom Plan is designed for learners who want a more flexible and personalized approach to improving their English skills. Whether you need to focus on conversational English, advanced grammar, or any other area of the language, the lessons are tailored to your specific goals and learning pace. Choose the frequency of your lessons and whether you prefer working with a native speaker or a qualified instructor.

What's Included

  • Tailored Lessons based on your personal learning needs.
  • Choose from standard or native speaker classes for more immersive learning.
  • Flexible class schedules that can be adjusted to fit your availability.
  • Conversation Practice for real-world communication.
  • Personalized coaching to help you meet your specific language goals.

Pricing

The Custom Plan is billed at $20 per hour for standard classes and $30 per hour for classes with a native speaker. You can select the number of hours and the frequency that best fit your schedule. There's no set package; you only pay for the classes you take.

Why Choose the Custom Plan?

  • Perfect for learners who need flexible, personalized lessons.
  • You control the pace, frequency, and type of classes you need.
  • Work with qualified instructors or native speakers for the most effective learning experience.
  • Ideal for learners who want a customized approach to improving specific skills.
